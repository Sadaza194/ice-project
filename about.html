Welcome to our tropical paradise! We are a family-owned and operated ice cream shop that specializes in creating unique and delicious tropical flavors.

Our journey began when our founders, Maria and Carlos, decided to combine their love for ice cream and their passion for the vibrant flavors and colors of the Caribbean. Maria, originally from Colombia, and Carlos, from the Dominican Republic, set out to create an ice cream experience that would transport their customers to a tropical oasis with every scoop.

Our team of experienced ice cream makers uses only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to craft our unique flavors. We source our fruits and spices from local markets and suppliers to ensure that our flavors are as authentic as possible. Our ice cream is made in small batches to ensure that each scoop is creamy, smooth, and bursting with flavor.

We offer a wide range of tropical flavors, including exotic flavors like guava, lulo, and Feijoa.

At our shop, we believe that ice cream is more than just a dessert; it's an experience. That's why we've created a warm and inviting atmosphere where our customers can relax, enjoy their ice cream, and escape the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope to see you soon at our shop, where every scoop is a taste of paradise!
